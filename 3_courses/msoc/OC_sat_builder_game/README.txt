Welcome to the OC Satellite Builder game.

This is a simple game for the demonstration of the contrasting requirements of trying to put 
together an ocean colour sensor package with a series of different "Missions" in mind.

Opening the index page with a web-browser should reveal a page with a picture of a satellite
on the right and a series of options for modules on the left. Your primary resources are weight
and money (budget) as all options have associated costs and masses.

Some options also have data and power requirements which will use up those resources.  These
resources can also be modified by choosing relevant options for the power system or data 
transfer system.

It is not possible to score 100% for any mission but try to get as high a score as you can. 
Think about what might be of most use for the mission selected (from the drop down tab) and 
hopefully you will come to an optimal solution.

Good luck!
